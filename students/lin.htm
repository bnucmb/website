RESEARCH & INTERESTS

Employing proteomic data of Escherichia coli, Saccharomyces cerevisiae, and Schizosaccharomyces pombe, genome-wide analyses protein sequences to discuss the relationship of protein evolution and elemental composition in amino acid.

Basing on high-throughput whole sequenced genomes, analyse amino acid compostion in different functional gene categories.

In addition, I also focus on problems about gene expression, functional categorization of genes and phylogenetic analysis. I think I will become interested in the project which needs the knowledge and technique of bioinformatics, computer science (like Web application) and statistics.
